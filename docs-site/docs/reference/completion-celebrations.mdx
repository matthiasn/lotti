# Customize completion celebrations

Open **Settings → Advanced Settings → Animations** to decide how Lotti responds
when you finish a task, complete a habit, or check off a checklist item.

<ManualScreenshot
  caseId="celebrations/controls"
  alt="Lotti Animations settings with celebration switches for habits, checklist items, tasks, and completion haptics"
  caption="Visual celebrations have a master switch and separate event switches. Completion haptics remain independent."
/>

## Choose where celebrations appear

- **Celebration animations** is the master switch for visual flourishes.
- **Habits**, **Checklist items**, and **Tasks** independently control their
  visual completion effects.
- **Completion haptics** controls the short physical response and remains
  available even when animations are disabled.

Turning off an animation never reverses the completion itself. If the operating
system requests reduced motion, Lotti suppresses the effects while preserving
your selected styles for later.

## Assign a style to each completion type

<ManualScreenshot
  caseId="celebrations/styles"
  alt="Lotti celebration style picker with Tasks selected and Sparks, Fireworks, Confetti, Embers, Bubbles, Random, and Combine two choices"
  caption="Tasks, habits, and checklist items can use different styles. Select a card to preview and apply it."
/>

For each completion type, choose **Sparks**, **Fireworks**, **Confetti**,
**Embers**, or **Bubbles**. Choose **Random** for a fresh single style on every
completion, or **Combine two** to layer two randomly selected styles.

Use **Try it** to preview the current selection without changing real task or
habit data.

## Tune an individual style

Select the customize action on a fixed style to open its full-screen
playground.

<ManualScreenshot
  caseId="celebrations/playground"
  alt="Lotti Sparks celebration playground with live task preview and grouped Shape, Motion, and Look controls"
  caption="Changes save and apply everywhere immediately. Replay the sample after each adjustment."
/>

The available controls depend on the effect and are grouped by **Shape**,
**Motion**, and **Look**. Adjust the sliders, then use the highlighted sample or
**Replay** to see the result. **Reset to default** restores the built-in style;
the confirmation offers **Undo** if the reset was accidental.

Customization is global for that style. It is not attached to the sample task
shown in the playground or to one particular real task.
