## Summary

- **3 implementation files are oversized** (`inference_provider_edit_page.dart` at 2362 lines is the most urgent; `ai_settings_page.dart` at 810 lines; `ai_config_card.dart` at 564 lines near threshold).
- **2 implementation files have no test file** (`util/ai_provider_status.dart` and `breakpoints.dart`).

- **6 test quality issues** are flagged: 1 zero-assertion test body (`'validates form fields'`), repeated `setSurfaceSize` boilerplate (67+ occurrences), inline GetIt setup violating the `setUpTestGetIt` rule, constructor smoke tests in the navigation service, a duplicate `createCompactWidget` helper, and a redundant `seededHarness`/`pumpSeeded` copy.

- **7 coverage / behavior gaps** identified, with `ai_provider_status.dart` (no tests at all), the FTUE-dismissed FAB branch, and the `AiConfigPrompt` fallback arm being the most impactful.
- **5 test execution speed wins** are available; eliminating the 114 `pumpAndSettle` calls in `inference_provider_edit_page_test.dart` and the 67+ per-test `setSurfaceSize` pairs are the highest-leverage changes.
