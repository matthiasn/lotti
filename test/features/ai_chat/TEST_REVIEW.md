## Recurring patterns

- **Fake-time policy violations.** `Future.delayed(ms)` and `Future.delayed(Duration.zero)` appear across services, controllers, and repository tests; the correct replacements (`fakeAsync`, `pumpEventQueue`, `async.flushMicrotasks`) are already used in other tests in the same files, demonstrating that the pattern is understood but inconsistently applied.
- **Inline mock / GetIt boilerplate instead of central helpers.** Inline `Mock` subclasses appear in `chat_recorder_controller_test.dart` (4 classes), `chat_interface_test.dart` (manual `GetIt.pushNewScope`), `chat_modal_page_test.dart`, `audio_transcription_service_test.dart`, and `task_summary_repository_test.dart`. All violate the centralized-mocks and `setUpTestGetIt()` rules.
- **Excess `pumpAndSettle` on non-settling animations.** `chat_interface_test.dart` (29), `chat_modal_page_test.dart` (3 on a repeating animation), `assistant_settings_sheet_test.dart` (8), and `message_bubble_test.dart` (5); the risk escalates when a `repeat()` animation is present.
- **Massive copy-paste mock stubs dwarfing test logic.** The `generate()` stub in `chat_repository_test.dart` (10 lines × 5 tests), `workEntriesInDateRange` stub in `task_summary_repository_test.dart` (12 lines × 9+ tests), and `createSession`/`saveSession`/`sendMessage` triple-stub in `chat_session_controller_test.dart` (10+ occurrences) are the dominant source of test-file inflation.
- **Weak assertions on meaningful behavior.** `toString()` smoke test, `isNotNull`-only timestamp check, `findsOneWidget`-only on `ChatInterface`, dead `Stopwatch` instrumentation with no enforced assertion — all confirm testing existence rather than behavior.
- **One-file-per-source rule violation.** Two separate test files cover `thinking_parser.dart`; this is the only confirmed violation but indicates that test placement was not consistently enforced during feature development.
