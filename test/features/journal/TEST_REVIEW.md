## Recurring patterns

- **Massively oversized test files mirroring complex impl files**: `entry_controller_test.dart` (3 034 ln for 571-ln impl), `journal_repository_test.dart` (2 426 ln for 401-ln impl), `entry_details_widget_test.dart` (2 349 ln for 673-ln impl) â€” each needs to be split at natural group/method boundaries.
- **One-test-file-per-source violations**: `journal_page_controller.dart` tested by 3 files, `entry_details_page.dart` by 4 files, `infinite_journal_page.dart` by 2 files, `modern_action_items.dart` by 4 files (1 main + 3 satellite), `create_entry_items.dart` by 2 files â€” the same root cause (gradual accretion of test groups into separate files) appears across every sub-directory.
- **Inline `getIt` registration bypassing `setUpTestGetIt()`**: Present in at least 7 test files across state and ui layers; the global `setUpAll` + `tearDownAll(getIt.reset)` pattern in `entry_controller_test.dart` is particularly dangerous for `very_good` batched run contamination.
- **`pumpAndSettle` overuse on non-animated widgets**: 84 calls in `modern_action_items_test.dart` alone for `ConsumerWidget`s with no animations; replicated in `action_menu_list_item_test.dart` (22), `initial_modal_page_content_test.dart` (15), and throughout the create/list_cards layers.
- **Real-time waits violating the fake-time policy**: 4-second `tester.pump(Duration(seconds:4))` in `modern_create_entry_items_test.dart`; 7Ă— `Future.delayed(Duration.zero)` in `entry_details_page_timer_highlight_test.dart`; `pumpWithDelay` 1.6 s Ă— 9 in `infinite_journal_page_test.dart`; retry-loop over-pump 4.2 s in `highlight_scroll_mixin_test.dart` â€” together adding ~25+ seconds to the journal test suite.
- **Smoke-only tests with no behavioral assertions**: `findsOneWidget` on the page type, `expect(true, isTrue)`, or constructor-echo checks appearing as the sole assertion in tests that claim to verify behavior â€” concentrated in `entry_details_page_edge_cases_test.dart` (5 of 8 tests), `entry_details_page_auto_scroll_test.dart` (3 tests), `modern_create_entry_items_test.dart` (multiple property-access tests).
- **Missing test files for pure-function utilities**: `entry_tools.dart` (5 pure functions), `entry_type_gating.dart`, `journal_page_subscriptions.dart`, `journal_page_state.dart`, `editor_styles.dart`, `editor_toolbar.dart`, `health_summary.dart`, `habit_summary.dart`, `save_button.dart` â€” a consistent gap across repository, state, and ui layers.
