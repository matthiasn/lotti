## Summary

- **3 oversized test files** above 800 lines (entities_cache: 1 533, db_notification: 1 214, vector_clock: 915) and 2 more above 800 lines (nav_service: 849, logging_service: 804). All are good Glados-first files that have grown large organically; generator scaffold extraction is the cleanest split.
- **3 impl files have no test file** (`dev_logger.dart`, `logging_domains.dart`, `share_service.dart`); `dev_logger` is the highest-value gap.
- **4 one-test-file-per-source violations**: `ip_geolocation_service.dart` (3 files), `screenshot_portal_service.dart` (2 files), `window_service_test.dart` hosts a stale `ServiceDisposer` group already covered by `service_disposer_test.dart`.
- **6+ weak/smoke tests** identified, most severely in `ip_geolocation_unit_test.dart` (three tests asserting constants against themselves at lines 82–110) and `db_notification_test.dart` line 207 (`isNotNull` + `isA<>` only).
- **No missing `tags: 'glados'`** — all 7 Glados tests in scope correctly carry the tag.
- **2 genuine Glados candidates**: `VectorClock` merge invariants and `LogDomain` structural contracts.
- **Biggest speed opportunity**: ~1 000 ms real wall-clock delay in `logging_service_test.dart` buffered-writing tests; replace with `flushAllForTest()` calls already present in adjacent tests.
